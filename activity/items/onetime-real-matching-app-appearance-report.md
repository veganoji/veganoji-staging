今回の動画では、「姉の彼氏を、目の前に並んだ男性20人の中から弟が本気で選ぶ」というユニークな設定のもと、年齢・仕事・収入・家事力まで含めて“本気審査”が行われる、緊張感たっぷりの内容となっています。恋愛リアリティーらしいドキドキ感はもちろん、兄弟ならではの本音や価値観も垣間見える構成で、最後まで見逃せない展開となっています。

こうした企画への出演を通して、ビーガン王子の活動の幅がさらに広がっていることも感じられます。メディアやSNSの枠を越え、さまざまなフィールドで新しい挑戦を続ける姿に、今後もぜひご注目ください。

### **English**

Vegan Oji appeared on the YouTube show “ONETIME” in its “Real Matching App” episode.

This episode features a bold and unusual concept: a younger brother seriously chooses his older sister’s boyfriend from a lineup of 20 men standing right in front of him. Framed as a “real matching app,” the participants are evaluated not only on appearance, but also on factors such as age, job, income, and even housekeeping ability. The result is a tense and entertaining episode that brings out genuine opinions, family dynamics, and unexpected compatibility.

Vegan Oji appears as one of the participants in this distinctive format, showing a different side of himself from his usual activities. Because the concept goes beyond looks alone and focuses on real-life qualities as well, the episode gives viewers a more natural sense of each participant’s personality and presence.

Appearances like this continue to highlight the growing range of Vegan Oji’s work. As he keeps taking on new challenges across different types of media and entertainment, this episode offers another glimpse into that expanding journey.
