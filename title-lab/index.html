どれにする？ / Which one?

For the chibi-sticker direction: v11 or v12 are my picks. v11 has the most cinematic structure — small "tagline" feel followed by a huge short question. v12 doubles down on the sticker language (both elements badged), which feels most aligned with the homepage social icons. v13 is the most minimal and high-impact but relies on surrounding copy to carry the context.

Among the earlier set: v2 (gold glow) and v4 (serif) were the strongest pure-CSS options. The generated v8/v9 had issues; can regen if needed.
