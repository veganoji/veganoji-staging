## ブランド・事業ポートフォリオ — Brands & ventures under this one entity
One company operates a "house of brands." Externally, the single legal entity is always
**ビーガン王子株式会社**; the brands below are its products/divisions.

- **Vegan Oji** — vegan-lifestyle D2C brand + chibi character; content, LINE stickers, vegan map.
- **OJI Entertainment** — talent / model agency (talent management, casting, entertainment).
- **OJI Digital** — internal tools & client portals (invoicing, contracts, distributor management).
- **InboxOji** — AI email-reply SaaS ("controlled automation, in your voice").
- **CastingOji** — AI email-replier for models.
- **AlmostJP** — bilingual paid-tools portal for a casting agency.
- **Japan Vegan Map** — vegan-spot map of Japan.

brand and a family of "OJI" digital and talent ventures — making veganism fun, delicious, and cute.
